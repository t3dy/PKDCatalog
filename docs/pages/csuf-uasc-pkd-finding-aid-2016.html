Philip K. Dick Manuscript Collection Finding Aid (CSUF, SC.6, 2016)

CSU Fullerton University Archives & Special Collections · 2016 · Scholarship

This is the 2016 finding aid for the Philip K. Dick Manuscript Collection (Collection SC.6) held at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF) University Archives and Special Collections. The collection spans bulk dates 1959 to 1985 and comprises 14 document boxes.

The finding aid specifies that the collection is open for research, though some materials are subject to access and reproduction restrictions. Property rights reside with CSUF, and no part of any manuscript may be quoted for publication without written permission from University Archives and Special Collections (800 N. State College, PLS-352, Fullerton, CA 92834-3599, phone 657-278-3444).

The CSUF Philip K. Dick Papers represent one of the two most important institutional repositories for Dick scholarship (alongside the Huntington Library holdings). The collection contains original manuscripts, correspondence, and related materials spanning Dick's career from the late 1950s through his death in 1982 and the subsequent management of his literary estate through 1985. This finding aid is an essential reference tool for any researcher planning to work with primary Dick materials, providing detailed descriptions of the collection's organization, access conditions, and the scope of holdings.
