SF Commentary, Issue 17

Bruce Gillespie (ed.) · c. 1970-1971 · Fan Publications

SF Commentary No. 17 is an issue of Bruce Gillespie's influential Australian science fiction critical fanzine. The issue includes a checklist of authors and works mentioned, referencing major science fiction figures including Poul Anderson, Isaac Asimov, John Bangsund, Algis Budrys, and John Campbell, among others. Works discussed include Anderson's Brake, The Man Who Came Early, The Moon Rakers, and Starfog, as well as references to the fanzine Warhoon.

The extensive checklist format indicates this issue contained wide-ranging critical coverage of the science fiction field. SF Commentary's practice of indexing its contents reflects Gillespie's commitment to creating a serious reference resource for science fiction criticism. The issue contributes to the broader critical ecosystem in which Dick's work was discussed and evaluated alongside other major science fiction authors of the period.
