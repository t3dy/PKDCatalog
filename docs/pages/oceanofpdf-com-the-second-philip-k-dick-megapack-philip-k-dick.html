The Second Philip K. Dick MEGAPACK

Philip K. Dick · 2015 · Short Stories · PKD-authored

The Second Philip K. Dick MEGAPACK is a large e-book anthology published by Wildside Press in 2015 as part of their popular MEGAPACK series. The volume contains thirteen stories: The Unreconstructed M, Sales Pitch, Shell Game, Breakfast at Twilight, Adjustment Team, Foster You're Dead, The Crawlers, The Golden Man, The Last of the Masters, The Turning Wheel, Upon the Dull Earth, Human Is, and Meddler.

Publisher John Betancourt's note acknowledges Dick as one of the iconic science fiction figures of the twentieth century whose reach now extends across books, movies, and television shows. The MEGAPACK series itself is described as Wildside Press's most popular endeavor. Cover art is by Dmytro Tolokonov via Fotolia.

This collection emphasizes Dick's prolific short fiction output from the 1950s, including several stories that have been adapted for screen: "Adjustment Team" became The Adjustment Bureau (2011), "Sales Pitch" and "Human Is" were adapted for Electric Dreams, and "The Golden Man" was loosely adapted as Next (2007). The volume serves as an affordable digital introduction to Dick's short fiction for readers discovering his work through film and television adaptations.
