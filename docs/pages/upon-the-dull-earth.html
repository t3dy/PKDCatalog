Upon the Dull Earth (Vocabulary Quiz / Study Guide)

Unknown (educator) · Other

This is a 5-page educational document containing a vocabulary quiz based on Philip K. Dick's short story "Upon the Dull Earth." The quiz format presents passages from the story followed by multiple-choice questions testing vocabulary comprehension.

The opening passage describes Silvia's words being drowned out by distant flapping sounds, like vast leaves rustling in a winter storm, as the night sky comes alive with frantic poundings. The creatures are described as greedy and desperately eager, with flickers of fear touching the man (Rick) as he runs to catch up with Silvia. The first vocabulary question tests comprehension of the word 'frantic' with options including calm and relaxed, very worried or rushed with urgency, extremely shiny, and careless.

"Upon the Dull Earth" is one of Dick's more unusual short stories, blending domestic drama with cosmic horror and theological themes. The story involves beings that descend from the sky to consume offerings, and its title likely alludes to a passage from Shakespeare. This educational document demonstrates how Dick's work has entered the curriculum, being used as teaching material for vocabulary and reading comprehension instruction.
