Research Note: Eric Foss Hour 25 Collection at UC Riverside (MS 054)

Unknown (archival research document) · 2025 · Other

This research document provides information about the Eric Foss collection related to the Hour 25 science fiction radio program. The collection was donated to the University of California, Riverside in 2011 and is cataloged as MS 054 within the Eaton Collection of Science Fiction and Fantasy at Tomas Rivera Library.

The document provides detailed contact information: UC Riverside Library, Special Collections and University Archives, Tomas Rivera Library, 4th Floor, P.O. Box 5900, Riverside, CA 92517-5900, phone (951) 827-3233, email specialcollections@ucr.edu. The finding aid notes that the collection is unprocessed, meaning contents have not been fully inventoried at the item level and there is no public listing of specific episodes included in the donation.

Hour 25 was a Los Angeles-based science fiction radio program that aired interviews with major SF authors. The program's archive is potentially significant for Dick studies as it may contain audio recordings of Dick appearances or discussions of his work. The document advises researchers to contact Special Collections directly to inquire about specific holdings, particularly regarding recordings from periods when Dick may have been featured on the program.
