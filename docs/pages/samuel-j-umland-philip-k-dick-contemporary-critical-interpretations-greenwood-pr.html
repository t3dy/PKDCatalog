Philip K. Dick: Contemporary Critical Interpretations

Samuel J. Umland (ed.) · 1995 · Scholarship

Philip K. Dick: Contemporary Critical Interpretations is an edited collection of scholarly essays compiled by Samuel J. Umland and published by Greenwood Press, a major academic publisher. At 120 pages, the volume collects contemporary scholarly readings of Dick's fiction from multiple critical perspectives.
