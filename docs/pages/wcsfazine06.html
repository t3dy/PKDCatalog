WCSFAzine #6 (February 2008)

West Coast Science Fiction Association · 2008 · Fan Publications

WCSFAzine No. 6 (February 2008) is the fannish e-zine of the West Coast Science Fiction Association (WCSFA), dedicated to promoting the West Coast science fiction community. The issue includes a feature on the most popular Canadian science fiction fanzine of the 1940s, connecting contemporary fan activity to the historical roots of Canadian science fiction fandom.

The West Coast Science Fiction Association is based in British Columbia, Canada, connecting this publication to the Vancouver science fiction community that hosted Dick's famous 1972 convention appearance. WCSFAzine represents the continuing vitality of the Pacific Northwest and Canadian science fiction fan community that intersected with Dick's life and legacy.

As a 2008 electronic publication, WCSFAzine illustrates the transition of science fiction fandom from print to digital formats. The publication's focus on community promotion and historical documentation makes it a valuable source for understanding the fan networks that have sustained interest in Dick's work, particularly in the Canadian and Pacific Northwest science fiction communities.
