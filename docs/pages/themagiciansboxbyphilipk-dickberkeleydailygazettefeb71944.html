The Magician's Box (Berkeley Daily Gazette, February 7, 1944)

Philip K. Dick · 1944 · Short Stories · PKD-authored

A single-page scanned PDF of 'The Magician's Box,' an early piece by Philip K. Dick published in the Berkeley Daily Gazette on February 7, 1944. No OCR text layer is present. Dick would have been approximately fifteen years old at the time of publication.

Like 'The Jungle People' (1942) and 'The Visitation' (1944), this is one of Dick's juvenile publications in the Berkeley Daily Gazette, documenting the formative years of his literary development. The title 'The Magician's Box' suggests themes of illusion, hidden realities, and transformation that would later become central preoccupations of his adult fiction. These juvenile publications are among the rarest and most sought-after items in Dick bibliography.
