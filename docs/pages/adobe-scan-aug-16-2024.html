Philip K. Dick Papers – Special Collections Finding Aid

Other

This document is a brief informational flyer or brochure about the Philip K. Dick Papers held in Special Collections at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF). The document describes Special Collections as housing the library's rarest and most valuable original materials.

Among the holdings referenced are the Boswell Collection on the History of Cartography, the Frederick and Patricia Shroyer Science Fiction, Fantasy, and Horror collection, and the James Boyer May (Amsberry-May) papers. The science fiction collections contain original manuscripts by Ray Bradbury, Philip K. Dick, and Frank Herbert, as well as materials related to the original Star Trek series.

The document directs researchers to finding aids available through the Online Archives of California (oac.cdlib.org) and the CSUF library catalog (library.fullerton.edu). CSUF's Special Collections is one of the most important institutional repositories for Dick scholarship, housing manuscripts, correspondence, and related materials that have been essential to biographical and scholarly work on Dick. The Dick Papers at Fullerton have been consulted by virtually every major Dick biographer and scholar.
