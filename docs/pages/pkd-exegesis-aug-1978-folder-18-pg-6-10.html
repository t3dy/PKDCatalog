PKD Exegesis: August 1978, Folder 18, Pages 6-10

Philip K. Dick · 1978 · Primary Sources · PKD-authored

Scanned pages 6 through 10 of Folder 18 of Philip K. Dick's Exegesis, dated August 1978. No OCR text layer is present. This document continues directly from the preceding scan of pages 1-5, representing the second half of a ten-page sequence from this folder.

Together with the first five pages, this scan provides a complete ten-page segment of Dick's ongoing philosophical journal from a single folder. The August 1978 date places these pages during a particularly intense period of Exegesis writing, as Dick was developing the theological and cosmological framework that would inform his VALIS trilogy. These primary source documents are essential for scholars seeking to trace the development of Dick's late metaphysical thought beyond what is available in the published edition.
