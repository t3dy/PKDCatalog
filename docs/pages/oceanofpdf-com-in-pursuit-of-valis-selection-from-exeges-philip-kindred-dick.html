In Pursuit of VALIS: Selections from the Exegesis (OceanofPDF edition)

Philip K. Dick (edited by Lawrence Sutin) · 1991 · Primary Sources · PKD-authored

This is a separate digital edition of In Pursuit of VALIS: Selections from the Exegesis, the same 1991 Underwood-Miller anthology also cataloged under id 41706725-dick-philip-k-in-pursuit-of-valis. This copy features cleaner OCR text compared to the other edition in the archive.

Edited by Lawrence Sutin, the volume collects selections from Philip K. Dick's Exegesis. The publication details confirm the same ISBN numbers (clothbound: 0-88733-091-6, softcover: 0-88733-093-2), with contributions from Sutin (preface), Terence McKenna (afterword), Jay Kinney (introduction), and Paul Williams (PKD Chronology).

The presence of two copies in the archive underscores its importance as a primary research document. Until the publication of the full Exegesis by Houghton Mifflin in 2011 (edited by Pamela Jackson and Jonathan Lethem), this Sutin-edited volume was the principal published source for Dick's late-period metaphysical writings recording his sustained philosophical investigations following his mystical experiences of February-March 1974.
